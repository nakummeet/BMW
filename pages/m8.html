THE M8

The BMW M8 Competition Coupé and the BMW M8 Coupé from 2019 onwards combined pure M genes with supreme exclusivity. Innovative technologies such as BMW M Mode and Adaptive M suspension ensure unparalleled driving dynamics. The M TwinPower Turbo 8-cylinder petrol engine with max. 460 kW (625 hp) guarantees performance in keeping with the latest standards. The BMW M8 is the perfect symbiosis of pure sportiness and exquisite luxury.

* with M Steptronic Sport transmission

M Power.

The M TwinPower Turbo 8-cylinder petrol engine guarantees performance in keeping with the latest standards. In the BMW M8 Competition Coupé, the high-revving unit develops an output of max. 460 kW (625 hp). The two turbochargers stand out thanks to their cross-cylinder bank exhaust manifold with optimum response characteristics. The V8 engine is also designed for extreme loads on the race track.

TECHNICAL DETAILS.

M Mode. The M MODE button on the centre console can adjust the control intensity of the driver assistance systems. The ROAD mode offers the full range of functions of the electronic assistants. In the SPORT setting, they only warn but do not intervene. Depending on the selected mode, the display network also shows the corresponding driving information. The BMW M8 Competition Coupé additionally features TRACK mode, which allows the driver to concentrate fully on the race track without any intervention. M xDrive. M xDrive with Active M Differential guarantees maximum traction and driving dynamics for everyday driving, as well as at the racetrack. The M specific technology combines the typical agility of a rear-wheel drive with the control of an all-wheel drive. There is a choice between the drive variants 4WD, 4WD Sport and – in DSC-off mode – 2WD. Adaptive M suspension. The Adaptive M suspension comes as a standard and makes it possible to adjust the damper characteristics to suit any given driving situation, increasing driving dynamics and comfort. In addition to the standard COMFORT setting for increased driving comfort, the SPORT program offers more direct damper settings, and SPORT+ for use on the racetrack. M Sport exhaust system. The M Sport exhaust system comes as a standard and delivers a dynamic sound that can be modified via the integrated exhaust valve mechanism. The SPORT and SPORT+ programmes, which can be selected via the driving dynamics buttons, are accentuated by an even more intense engine sound, while the comfort-oriented programme delivers a more discreet sporty sound.
